About Zero-to-Hackathon Copilot

What is Zero-to-Hackathon Copilot?

AI teammate that takes your rough idea and creates team roles, build plan, pitch script, and demo checklist for 24-48 hour hackathons.

How It Works

  • Input your hackathon idea.
  • Get an auto roadmap and task board.
  • Generate a 3-minute pitch and demo checklist.

Who Uses It?

  • Hackathon participants
  • College tech clubs
  • First-time builders
