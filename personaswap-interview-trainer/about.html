About PersonaSwap Interview Trainer

What is PersonaSwap Interview Trainer?

AI conducts mock interviews as different interviewer personalities (friendly, aggressive, technical). Practice with voice/text Q&A and get feedback scorecards.

How It Works

  • Choose an interviewer mode: Friendly, Aggressive, Technical.
  • Answer questions in voice or text.
  • Receive instant feedback and a scorecard.

Who Uses It?

  • Job seekers
  • Students
  • Bootcamp graduates
