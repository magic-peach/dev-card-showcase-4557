About ShadowSkill Marketplace

What is ShadowSkill Marketplace?

A platform where people learn by “shadowing” experts live while they work (coding, design, marketing, editing).

How It Works

  • Browse expert session listings.
  • Join live screen + voice sessions.
  • Take notes and bookmark timestamps.

Who Uses It?

  • Students
  • Freelancers
  • Career switchers
