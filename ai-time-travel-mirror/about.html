About AI Time-Travel Mirror

What is AI Time-Travel Mirror?

This web app lets you upload a selfie, choose a future life path, and see a generated “future self” avatar, lifestyle plan, and daily routine simulation. It helps you visualize long-term goals in a concrete, motivating way.

How It Works

  • Upload your photo and pick a future path.
  • The app generates a future avatar, lifestyle plan, and 30-day action plan.
  • See your simulated dashboard and track your progress.

Why Use It?

  • Visualize your goals and stay motivated.
  • Get a concrete roadmap for self-improvement.
  • Share your future profile with friends or coaches.
