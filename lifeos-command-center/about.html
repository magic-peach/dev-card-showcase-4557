About LifeOS Command Center

What is LifeOS Command Center?

A personal dashboard that unifies goals, money, fitness, calendar, and learning into one adaptive control panel.

How It Works

  • Connect two data sources (calendar, fitness, finance, learning).
  • Track weekly goals and progress.
  • Get AI suggestions for your next best action.

Who Uses It?

  • Students
  • Founders
  • Creators
  • Remote workers
